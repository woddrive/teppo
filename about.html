About Teppo

Introducing Teppo, a training companion designed for athletes competing in the HYROX. Teppo is a fitness application that provides cross-training workouts designed specifically to enhance athletic performance as it relates to the physical demands of the competition. Workouts consist primarily of the movements that are encountered during the event and are structured in a variety of formats that are structured in ways to improve strength, speed, endurance, and overall work capacity. Teppo has the workouts you need to take your fitness to the next level and crush your competition.
