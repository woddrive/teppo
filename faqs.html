Frequently Asked Questions


Is Teppo suitable for beginners?
Absolutely! The HYROX Workout Generator and related features cater to individuals of all fitness levels, including beginners. You can try a workout as is and go at your own pace, or customize the workout details and create your own unqiue training session.

Can I use the HYROX workout generator on my smartphone?
Yes, Teppo is designed to be mobile-friendly. You can access it on your smartphone or tablet, allowing you to carry instant workouts wherever you go.

How often should I use Teppo's workout generator?
Short answer - as often as you like! It is recommended to use Teppo as a companion to a properly organized training plan.

Can I customize the workouts according to my preferences?
While the workout generator provides pre-designed workouts, it also allows you to tailor the exercises to your liking. You can modify the duration, volume, and specific exercises to suit your specific training objectives.

Can I integrate workouts with other fitness tracking apps?
At the moment, Teppo operates as a standalone tool. However, you can easily copy a workout and input the details into other fitness tracking apps or devices for comprehensive monitoring.

Can I share workouts on social media?
Absolutely! Please do! The workout generator tools allow you to share content on various social media platforms. You can share with your peers or training clients, use it to post daily workouts and inspire others, or to simply connect with the HYROX community.
